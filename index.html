What is SETU?

SETU is a high-efficiency, low-bandwidth smart learning platform designed to bridge the education gap for rural colleges. It enables students to access quality lectures, quizzes, and learning materials even on slow internet connections.

Our mission is simple — Quality education should not depend on internet speed.

The Education Gap Challenge

📶 Infrastructure Issues

Many rural areas lack stable 4G / 5G internet connectivity.

💰 Economic Constraints

Rural institutions cannot afford expensive learning platforms.

📱 Device Limitations

Most apps fail on low-end smartphones used by students.

🎓 Skill Gap

Students lack access to specialist lecturers and modern resources.

Our Smart Solution

🎧 Audio First Learning

Clear audio even on slow networks.

🌐 Progressive Web App

Runs on any device without heavy downloads.

📦 Resilient Downloads

Resume learning even if internet disconnects.

⚡ QoS Optimized Streaming

Lag-free lecture delivery on unstable networks.
